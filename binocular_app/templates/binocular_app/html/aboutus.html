About

Binocular is a free learning platform with simplified examples for optimized learning .

Coding Examples are regularly reviewed to aviod errors and provide latest information.

Binocular was created in 2024 and is , and always will be a free developer resourse platform which focuses on simplified easy learning.

Using heaps of examples and exercises which start from basic and lead up professional scenarios.

Binocular are keenly dadicated to provide heaps of examples to help to understand complex concepts in a simplifiied manner.

You can also try our online editor Workboard and work on your custom scenarios and test its workings before using them in your projects.

You can help us ensure Binocular remains updated by reporting us any issues that popup using the "Feedback" button on the sidebar.

If you wish to help Binocular get discovered by more individual then feel free to specify us in your projects or by shareing our links to others.
